📋 Methodology

The Job Market Crash Index is calculated using a composite algorithm that analyzes multiple real-time signals related to AI-driven workforce displacement, automation proliferation, hiring trends, and economic disruption indicators.

📰 Layoff Tracking

NLP analysis of global news for layoff announcements, hiring freezes, and workforce reductions

🤖 Automation Velocity

Tracking AI/robotics research output and new model releases that automate human tasks

🧰 Tool Proliferation

Monitoring the rate of new AI tools and models entering the market

📉 Economic Signals

Aggregating unemployment indicators, hiring sentiment, and labor market disruption data
